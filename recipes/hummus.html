Real Hummus

Hummus

A family recipe, from ROYHOBBS.

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, divided
  • 1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, half the liquid reserved
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. In a blender, chop garlic.
  2. Pour garbanzo beans into the blender, reserving about 1 tablespoon for garnish.
  3. Add reserved liquid, lemon juice, tahini, and salt to the blender.
  4. Blend until creamy and well mixed.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a medium serving bowl.
  6. Sprinkle with pepper and pour olive oil over the top.
  7. Garnish with reserved garbanzo beans.
