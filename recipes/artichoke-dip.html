Luscious Spinach Artichoke Dip

Spinach Artichoke Dip

An artichoke dip for any party.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • ½ (10 ounces) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup cream cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon minced garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a small baking dish, mix together artichoke hearts, spinach, sour cream, mayonnaise, cream cheese, Romanao cheese, and garlic. Cover dish.
  3. Bake until heated through and bubbly, about 25 minutes.
