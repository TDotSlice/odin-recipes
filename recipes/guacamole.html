Easy Guacamole

Guacamole Party

A quick and simple recipe for delicious guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel and smash avacados in a medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, garlic, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Season with remaining lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill for half an hour to blendn flavors.
